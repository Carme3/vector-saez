Artistname:

Carmela Saez

My favorite pet animal is cat,

because cats are lovely and cute.

My cat name is Akane.

My father adopted Akane from the rice field

when he saw him crying and homeless.

Since then we have been taking care of Akane very well.

Akane is a jealous cat and he hate other cats.

But he loves everyone in our house including our pet dog, Spy.

The latest weight of Akane is 4.5 kilograms.

The favorite thing I like about Akane is

he never hurt people with his nails.

He likes sleeping near me during day time.

Everyone in my family likes Akane.
